Skin Aging

Healthy skin protects us from the external environment and prevents moisture loss.

At any age, healthy skin feels smooth, firm, moisturized and has even coloration.


As we age, our skin encounters various afflictions such as dryness, dark circles under eyes, puffy eyes, fine lines, deep wrinkles, enlarged pores, dull skin tone, pigmentation, loss of firmness, etc. The aging process accelerates when skin is exposed to the following conditions:


1 , UV rays, environmental pollution and climate change.

2 , Work and stress, plus a hectic lifestyle with inadequate sleep.

3 , Unhealthy habits such as alcohol drinking, smoking, overeating, etc.
